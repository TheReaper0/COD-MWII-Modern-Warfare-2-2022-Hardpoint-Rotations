Call Of Duty MW2 Modern Warfare II 2022 Hardpoint Rotation Reference Images

Collection of jpgs that show the location and order of the Hardpoint rotations for MWII 2022 maps.

I'll add new DLC maps when I get the chance.

Click on Green "code" button and "download zip" to download all images to your local PC or use in situ for reference.
